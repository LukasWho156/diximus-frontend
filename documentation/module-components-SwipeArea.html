Class: SwipeArea

components~SwipeArea(props)

Wrap a component in a `div` that can be swiped to the left or to the right on mobile devices. When the area is swiped by more than 10% of the screen width, invoke the provided `onSwipeLeft` or `onSwipeRight` methods, respectively.
